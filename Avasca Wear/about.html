About Us

History

Avasca Clothing Line was launched in the year 2019 as a streetwear brand dedicated to coming up with unique styles for the people interested in looking and feeling good.On the first year of launching Avasca, the company was able to scale it in a slow but steady manner that saw the brand gain more recognition mostly among the younger generation.
The second year brought more blessing to the company as the brand became more popular and received national recognition not only from fashion icons but also leaders in various fields. Growth

The brand's growth has not been very fast but has been steadily increasing. The brand continues to grow each year as our data has shown us. The company has been interested in building a brand that resonates strongly with the audience, thus, the slow but sure growth has been a blessing for the brand.
The main goal is to build something that the customers will be proud of showing to their fellow colleagues, friends and the public.Thus, the company aims at steadily growing a loyal customer base which will continue being the customers even later in the future.

Future

Avasca Clothing Line is very optimistic about the future. Going by our progress, we are confident that Avasca is continuing to grow rapidly and the future is looking more brighter than ever. We have several plans lined up for the near and later future. These plans include collaboration with other clothing in order to not only grow our company but to grow the entire industry in general.
These collaborations will see us gain more customers in new geographical regions as well as introduce our client base to other cool clothing lines doing amazing stuffs. Such initiatives will benefit all the parties involved and in turn make the industry to be more unified for the betterment of all.
